About Us

We are a small team of Cyber Security students who collaborated on this website as a class project for the subject of Human and Computer Interactions. Our team believes that the intersection of technology and user experience is critical, especially in the rapidly evolving field of cyber security. This project allowed us to apply both theoretical knowledge and practical skills to create something meaningful.

The primary purpose of this website is to provide users with the latest news related to cyber security. We strive to ensure that our content is up-to-date, covering topics such as data breaches, vulnerabilities, exploits, and the latest trends in the cyber world. By focusing on these areas, we aim to keep users informed about critical developments in the field.

Additionally, this website features a section dedicated to suspected attackers. Users can access detailed profiles of individuals or groups linked to cyber threats. These profiles are carefully curated to help users understand the motivations, methods, and potential risks posed by these attackers, thereby raising awareness and promoting proactive measures.

Through this project, we hope to foster a deeper understanding of cyber security among our audience. By blending educational content with practical insights, our platform is designed to be a reliable resource for students, professionals, and enthusiasts in the cyber security community. This is just the beginning, and we aim to improve and expand the website further as we continue our journey in this fascinating field.
